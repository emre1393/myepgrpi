For Windows:
1. Enable the lines Marked *Windows
2. Disable the lines marked *Linux
Just use the ini file, like any other ini file.

For Linux:
1. Put the two php files into the root public folder on your web server.
2. Put the ini file into WebGrab directory, just like any other ini files.
3. Enable the lines Marked *Linux
4. Disable the lines marked *Windows
